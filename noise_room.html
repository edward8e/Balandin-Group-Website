Noise Room

ROOM MSE 254

900 University Ave.

Riverside, CA 92521

email
Tel: 951.827.2351
Fax: 951.827.2425

Noise room is designated for the electronic device characteristics measurement, and noise performance measurement. The equipment are list as following.

  • Lakeshore TTPX cryogenic probe station equipped with six probes. Temperature range: 77K to 480K.
  • Agilent B1500 Semiconductor parameter analyzer with 8 SMUs.
  • Standford Research 785 Dynamic Signal Analyzer.
  • Standford Research 560 Low Noise Amplifier.
  • Home-build biasing circuit for noise measurement.
