Biography

Adane Geremew, studied Nanotechnology at the University of Barcelona department of Physics from 2010 - 2013. Continued research, as a research assistant, in 2D materials for device development at the university of Texas at Austin. Since September 2016, Adane joined Prof Alexander Balandin group as a graduate student and is currently working on charge density wave materials for device development.
